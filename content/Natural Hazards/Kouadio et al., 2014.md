# Infectious diseases following natural disasters: prevention and control measures

Outbreaks of infectious diseases may be reported when the earthquake disasters result in substantial population displacement into unplanned and overcrowded shelters, with limited access to food and safe water. Disease outbreaks may also result from the destruction of water/sanitation systems and the degradation of sanitary conditions directly caused by the earthquake
