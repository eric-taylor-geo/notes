# Real-time earthquake monitoring for Tsunami Warning System inPetropavlovsk-Kamchatsky (Russian Far East) during 2011-2015

- Tsunami Warning System (TWS) in Kamchatka was established in Petropavlovsk-Kamchatsky, Russia in 1956–1959 years as a direct result of the great earthquake and tsunami that occurred on November 05, 1952 (M = 8.5). In2006–2010 TWS in the Russian Far East was deeply modernized as the response of Federal officials to the tragedyof December 26, 2004. The task of the Kamchatka Branch of the Geophysical Survey was to develop and implement the seismological components (Chebrov et al., 2009, 2012).
