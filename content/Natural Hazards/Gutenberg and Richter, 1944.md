# Frequency of earthquakes in California

B-value paper

The Gutenberg-Richter law expresses the relationship between the magnitude and total number of earthquakes in a given region and time period of at least that magnitude. It is expressed as:
$$
\log_{10}N = a - bM
$$
or
$$
N = 10^{a-bM}
$$
where
- $N$ is the number of events having a magnitude $≥ M$,
- $a$ and $b$ are constants.

$b$ is typically close to unity.
