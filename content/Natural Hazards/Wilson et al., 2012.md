# Volcanic ash impacts on critical infrastructure

- Paper focuses on following critical infrastructure: electricity generation, transmission and distribution; water supply; waste water; telecommunications; and transportation systems

**Airport Case Study: Guatemala City

- Airport closure of Guatemala International Airport (La Aurora) on 28 May 2010 - re-opened on 1 June 2010
- Main reason for closure was to allow for the clean up of the airport
- Required additional staff - loaned by the army and airforce
- Cleanup involved bulldozers and shovelling into trucks

**Marine Transportation:**

- Marine transportation is affected by ash in three main ways: direct physical impacts from falling ash, visibility disruption, physical impacts from pumice rafts.
