# Guidelines on Urban Disaster Scenarios Development and their Application for Risk Control

The economic stability index of this area (Sd) is 1,5 that is not too high. The degree of disaster’s economic impact on the SPUR (Dm) is 5.5 that is higher than 5 i.e. this area cannot withstand such disaster with its own capacity. Hence, the disaster is beyond the SPUR capacity and has the national (Russia) scale. The disaster of such a size is not permissible and the urgent measures for the disaster mitigation are necessary to be undertaken.
