# Products and dynamics of lava-snow explosions: The 16 March 2017 explosion at Mount Etna, Italy

On 16 March 2017, during a mildly effusive and explosive eruption at Mount Etna, Italy, a slowly advancing lava lobe interacted with the snow cover to produce a sudden, brief sequence of explosions

Material struck nearby tourists, resulting in injuries.

Mitigation measures should be established to prevent potentially dramatic accidents at worldwide volcanoes frequented by tourists and with fairly easy access, such as Etna
