#  Seismic risk assessment and mapping at different levels

In the Russian Federation, we have more than 20 million people living in regions of moderate and high seismicity. The areas that are hit by earthquakes, with events of intensity VII (MMSK-86 scale) or greater occurring, make up approximately 20% of Russia’s area

Examples of more detailed risk assessment at urban level for Petropavlovsk-Kamchatsky City and Sochi City are given. In order to verify the vulnerability of buildings’ stock, various sources of information are used. In the paper, two methods to get data are analyzed: building by building land inspection and joint usage of high-resolution space images and web panoramas.
