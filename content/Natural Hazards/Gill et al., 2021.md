# Workshop Report: Multi-Hazard Risk Scenarios for Tomorrow’s Cities

Citation for dynamic risk

Original risk equation is:
$$
\text{Risk} = \text{Hazard} \times \text{Exposure} \times \text{Vulnerability}
$$
However, each of these interrelates with eachother and varies temporally and spatially, thus, dynamic risk is:
$$
\text{Risk} = f\left[\text{Hazard, Exposure, Vulnerability, Time}\right]
$$
