# Estimation of loss caused by earthquakes and secondary technological hazards
## 3. Seismic risk assessment at different levels

- Earthquakes are the most hazardous natural processes in the Russian Federation
- Seismic risk assessments considering secondary hazards are essential for planning and implementing preventative measures at national and local authority levels
- Many oil pipe routes cross earthquake-prone areas

## Scenario earthquake consequences

- Stravopol region is characterised by high density population with high level of seismic hazard - links with P-K
- Ran several earthquake scenarios under worst conditions (95% of population indoors - at night)
- In Stavropol City located 5 km from the epicentre of the scenario event, the seismic intensity may reach I = 6.5−7 (MMSK-86 scale). In this city about 30 % of buildings may undergo moderate damage (d = 2), about 20 % – heavy damage (d = 3) and about 2 % – partial collapse (d = 4).
