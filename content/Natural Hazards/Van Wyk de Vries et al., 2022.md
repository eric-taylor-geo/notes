# A massive rock and ice avalanche caused the 2021 disaster at Chamoli, Indian Himalaya


Catastrophic landslide in Chamoli in February 2021 was caused by a rock and ice avalanche which was able to travel far down the valley due to a combination of its high initial starting point, providing it with GPE, and its rock:ice ratio (80:20) which enabled to melting of ice through friction, which created a highly mobile debris flow which killed 200 people and severly damaged two hydropower plants.
