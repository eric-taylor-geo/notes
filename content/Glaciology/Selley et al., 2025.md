# Speed-up, slowdown, and redirection of ice flow on neighbouring ice streams in the Pope, Smith, and Kohler region of West Antarctica

- Kohler West ice stream has seen a slowdown due to Kohler East ice stream flowing and thinning faster, causing it to steal ice from Kohler West by redirecting ice.
- Occurred within 18 years
- Kohler West slowed by 10% between 2005 and 2022.
- Compared to an average speed up of 51%.
