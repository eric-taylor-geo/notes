#  Impact of a global temperature rise of 1.5 degrees Celsius on Asia’s glaciers

Here we show that a global temperature rise of 1.5 °C will lead to a warming of 2.1 ± 0.1 °C in HMA, and that 64 ± 7 per cent of the present-day ice mass stored in the HMA glaciers will remain by the end of the century.
