# Fast retreat of Zachariæ Isstrøm, northeast Greenland

Zacharia Glacier is one of two glaciers which drain the northeast Greenland ice stream, which drains 12% of the Greenland Ice Sheet ([[Rignot and Mouginot, 2012]]).
The NEGIS is the only large dynamic feature which extends continuously deep to the ice sheet's interior.
