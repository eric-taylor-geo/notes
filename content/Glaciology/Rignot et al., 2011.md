# Ice Flow of the Antarctic Ice Sheet

**Summary:**

Rignot _et al_. use deformation laws akin to Glen’s Flow Law to estimate surface ice velocity. Ice thickness was estimated using surface elevation models and base elevation models. Their results largely mirror the patterns of observed velocities using remote sensing. However, in several key areas (e.g., ice shelves and margins), their predictions were vastly different, suggesting that other mechanisms are at play.
