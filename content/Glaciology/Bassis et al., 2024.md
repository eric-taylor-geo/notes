# Review: Stability of Ice Shelves and Ice Cliffs in a Changing Climate

**Section 4**

- Originally inferred that MICI was necessary to explain past variations in SLr
- However subsequent work [[Edwards et al., 2019]] finds that there are large uncertainties
- No direct observations and most evidence comes from palaeoglacioogical evidence - iceberg scour marks in the Amundsun Sea Embayment consistent with rapid, full-thickness calving [[Wise et al., 2017]]
- Palaeoglacilogical evidence finds that grounding lines have retreated much more rapidly than what is currently observed [[Batchelor et al., 2023]]
