#  Melt-induced speed-up of Greenland ice sheet offset by efficient subglacial drainage

- SAR feature tacking - velocities of 6 glaciers over 5 years (‘93 & ‘95-’98) (relatively low elevations)
- Warm summers (a) – early summer speed up but late summer slowdown
- Cool summers (b) – early summer speed up but sustained in late summer
- Net result – greater summer flow in cool years! • Due to channelization, drop in pw & ¯ sliding in high melt years
