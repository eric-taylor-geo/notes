# Cryo-Hydrologic Warming: A potential mechanism for rapid thermal response of ice sheets

- Presence of liquid water in the cryo-hydrologic system from surface melt warms ice.
- Increasing temperatures resulting from cryo-hydrologic warming will reduce ice viscosity and thus contribute to faster ice flow
