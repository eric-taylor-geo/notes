# Ice flow modulated by tides at up to annual periods at Rutford Ice Stream, West Antarctica

- 2 years of GPS data 40 km upstream of Rutford Ice Stream's grounding line.
- Find a clear modulation of ice flow at semi-diurnal, diurnal, two-weekly, semi-annual, and annual ocean tidal frequencies.
- First observation of ice stream flow variations over such a long period and wide range of frequencies.
