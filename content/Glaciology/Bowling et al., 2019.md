# Distribution and dynamics of Greenland subglacial lakes

Three zones of lake formation:
- Stable lakes in the Northern and Eastern regions above the ELA, but away from the interior
- Hydrologically active lakes near the ELA, recharged by surface melt water
- Small, seasonally-active lakes below the ELA, form over winter and drain during the melt season.

Observations provide important constraints on the Greenland Ice Sheet's basal thermal regime.
