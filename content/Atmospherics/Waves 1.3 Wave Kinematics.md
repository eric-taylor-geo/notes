Phase speed: 

- Phase is $(lx + my - \omega t + \phi)$ 
- A given point on the wave (e.g., a crest) moves such that the phase remains constant:
	- Therefore, as $t$ changes through time, $x$ and $y$ must change in a way to keep the phase constant - track the feature moving through space.
- Therefore, $d/dt$ = 0
$$
l\frac{dx}{dt}+m\frac{dy}{dx}-\omega = 0
$$
