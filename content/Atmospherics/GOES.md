- NOAA's Geostationary Operational Environmental Satellites-R Series is the WH's most sophisticated weather-observing and environmental-monitoring system.
- Provide imagery, atmospheric measurements, space weather
- GOES-R Series imager scans the Earth 5x faster and 4x the resolution over 3x the number of channels than older GOES satellites.
- Can provide images as frequently as every 30 seconds
- GOES-R hosts a series of instruments for the detection of approaching space weather hazards, which can disrupt power, communication and other satellites.
- Instruments provide advanced imaging of the sun and detection of solar eruptions as well
