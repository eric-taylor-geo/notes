- Deep Space Climate Observatory
- Launched Feb 2015 from Cape Canaveral
- Succeeded NASA's Advanced Composition Explorer's role in supporting solar wind alerts and warnings from the L1 orbit
- L1 point is a good position because solar wind takes up to an hour to reach Earth from L1
- 15-60minute advanced warmings before storms of particles or magnetic fields from coronal mass ejections
