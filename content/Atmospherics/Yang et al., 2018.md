# EPIC Spectral Observations of Variability in Earth's Global Reflectance

- Paper presents initial EPIC shortwave spectral observations of the sunlit Earth reflectance
- Earth's reflectance depends mostly on:
	- Ratio between land and ocean areas exposed to the Sun
	- Cloud spatial and temporal distributions over the sunlit side of the Earth
		- Diurnal variations are determined by periodic changes in the land-ocean fraction
		- Daily reflectance displays a seasonal variation which is significant even without including the contribution of snow and ice from polar regions
		- Seasonal variations of sunlit Earth reflectance are mostly determined by the latitudinal distribution of oceanic clouds
